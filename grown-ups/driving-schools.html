Driving Schools

Ensuring Quality of Drivers in Rajasthan

Rajasthan Knowledge Corporation Limited (RKCL) offers IT enabled education programs to develop computer skills. RKCL caters 6 to 7 lacs students every year in various affiliated centers across the state. Looking at this enormous reach, RKCL in collaboration with Department of Transport & Road Safety, Government of Rajasthan has developed a road safety education module to create awareness among students.
