Welcome to Road Safety Portal

A Universal Repository on Road Safety

Rajasthan Knowledge Corporation Limited (RKCL) offers IT enabled education programs to develop computer skills. RKCL caters 6 to 7 lacs students every year in various affiliated centers across the state. Looking at this enormous reach, RKCL in collaboration with Department of Transport & Road Safety, Government of Rajasthan has developed a road safety education module to create awareness among students. With the vision to enhance outreach, RKCL has designed this comprehensive portal on under its Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) initiative. It is first dedicated portal on road safety in India. Through this portal, general public and government stakeholders can find intutive content on road safety including short films, self-paced learning modules,road safety laws & regulations, articles, safety tips and much more just on a click.

Road Crash Snapshot of Rajasthan (2021)

Rajasthan is the largest state in the country and accounts for approximately 10% of the total land area. More than ten thousand people die in approximately 22,000 road crashes every year in Rajasthan. According to number of road crash fatalities, Rajasthan ranks fifth in the nation. The severity rate, i.e. deaths per hundred accidents, in the state is around 47% which is very high as compared to the national severity rate. Take Road Safety Pledge

Road Safety is a collaborative responsibility. Being a responsible citizen it is our moral responsibility to pay respect to all road users and share the road. Government along with other stakeholders is working to improve the road safety scenario in the state but it cannot bring the change without involvement of the community.


Transport Dept. on Social Media

Department of Transport & Road Safety Rajasthan is committed to promote road safety through all mediums including the social media. Watch department's initiavie on use of social media for road safety.

Tax Wavier for E-Vehicles

E-vehicles is the future of transportation. Use of clean fuel and alternative sources of energy is essential to reduce carbon footprint. The department has exempted taxes for purchase of e-vehicles.

Road Safety Awareness

Awareness is the key to enhance outreach of initiatives among community. Transport Department along with stakeholders create awareness of road safety on print, electronic and social media platforms.
